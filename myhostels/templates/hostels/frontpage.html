Stairway offers students convenient, less costly, faster and easier way of booking rooms in a particular hostel of their choice in their various institutions. We offer hostel managers as well the ease of managing their database.The system is meant to provide services that would reduce difficulties in locating hostels. It also meant to reduce stress and reduce the high cost involved in booking rooms in hostels manually. Hostel managers on the other hand would be relieved of the burden and stress of assigning rooms to their students and managing a large amount of data manually.
